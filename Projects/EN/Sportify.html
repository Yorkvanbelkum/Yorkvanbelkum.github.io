Project 1

Sportify

This project was about a company that collects different gyms so you can see them all in one place and buy your subscription.

This is where I first discoverd the parallax effect.

The parallax effect is a kind of illusion thats magicly changes the background the further you scroll.

What I learned?

I started in this project experimenting with parallax effect and I really liked how it looked so I experimented some more. I also found out about "ID" and linked them with "a href" so you could have a nice navbar just like the one on this website.
